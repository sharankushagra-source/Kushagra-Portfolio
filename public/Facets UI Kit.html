The direction

Dark, editorial, and unafraid of color. A signature thread of six hot hues recurs across calm neutral walls, letting big serif questions and framed art carry each surface.

How to use

Pull any token or component below into a new layout. Keep the three type families, one hot color per surface, and the ink/paper base — that's what holds every variation together.

Built on

Bricolage Grotesque (display) · Instrument Serif (the questions) · Archivo (everything functional). Ink #100D08, Paper #F1EEE7.
